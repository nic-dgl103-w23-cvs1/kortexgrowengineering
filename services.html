Services

Kortex Grow Engineering has a specializes in the manufacture of top-quality CNC products and Lathe machine products. Our state-of-the-art CNC products are designed to meet the highest standards of precision, accuracy and reliability. These products are ideal for a wide range of industrial and commercial applications, including metalworking, woodworking, and plastic machining. Our Lathe machine products are renowned for their durability and versatility, and are highly sought after by machine shops, tool and die makers, and manufacturing facilities around the world. With a focus on innovation, we constantly strive to enhance our products, and are dedicated to providing our customers with the best possible solutions to meet their needs.

Production Services

CNC Machine

We have three dynamic CNC machines, each with eight robotic arms, that can act as a router, drill press, jigsaw, table saw, jointer, or even a surface planer.

Lathe Machine

Kortex has six semi-automatic lathe machines for woodturning, metalworking, metal spinning, thermal spraying, parts reclamation, and glass-working.

Welding Machine

The team of experts at Kortex has vast experience in six different methods of welding, including SMAW, GTAW, GMAW, FCAW, SAW, ESW, PAW, and Resistance Welding.

Laser Cutting

Having all three types of laser, CO2 , Fiber lasers, and YAG cutters allows us to fullfil major requirements of our clints and saves a considerable amount of time.

Grinder Machine

Kortex is the only company having Sand Grinder in local area providing Finishing of metal components in the manufacturing of machines and tools.

Planer Machine

Having three 4 Ton Matel Planning machine is our major production area, having more than 8000 clients all over the India.
